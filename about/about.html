Here, we study consciousness shifting with light and form.

I'm trained in architectural and interior design, visual art, dance, and comparative literature at colorado college - academy of art, san francisco - cornish college of the arts, seattle - emily carr school of art and design, vancouver - university of new mexico.
My specialties include custom fabrication and design services, glass-lamination and illumination, lighting - led, and sustainable wood.

Projects range from mixed use commercial to salons, restaurants, bars, and clubs, to residences throughout New Mexico, Colorado, California, and New York. I've been featured in trend art and architecture, panache, albuquerque arts, su casa, albuquerque journal, the magazine, the district, new at home, and club systems international; and in person at santa fe convention center, eileen braziel fine arts, miami art and design, hamptons art exhibit, la art fair, 516 arts, and klaudia marr project space.
